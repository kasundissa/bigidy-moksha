About Moksha

Ideal Motors unveiled the 'Ideal Moksha', Sri Lanka's first home-grown fully electric car. Drawing inspiration from the classic Mini Moke, Ideal Moksha is geared to be a game changer for the Sri Lankan market and the most practical solution in the current technology transition phase towards a sustainable, carbon-neutral future.
